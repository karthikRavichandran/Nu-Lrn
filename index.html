Who we are

Bridging maternal health and education with technology.

Nurture & Learn Foundation is a non-profit initiative that focuses on the first 1,000 days of life — from pregnancy to early childhood — and the learning years that follow.

Millions of mothers and children still lack access to timely health guidance and quality education. We believe AI can help skilled professionals reach more families, without replacing the human care that truly matters.

Our work sits at the intersection of:

  • Maternal & fetal health awareness and screening
  • Foundational education for children in low-resource settings
  • AI tools that personalise support, in local languages
What we aim to do in the first phase:
  • Run regular maternal health & fetal-care camps.
  • Set up after-school learning hubs for children.
  • Build simple AI-powered assistants for mothers & learners.

Our programs

Two pillars, one mission.

We focus on practical, on-the-ground programs that can be scaled with data and technology.

Pillar 1

Maternal & fetal health

Supporting mothers before, during and after pregnancy with basic care and timely information.

  • Pre-natal & post-natal check-up camps
  • Anaemia & nutrition screening
  • High-risk pregnancy identification and referral
  • Newborn care & breastfeeding counselling
Pillar 2

Children’s education

After-school and weekend sessions that strengthen core skills and nurture curiosity.

  • Maths, reading and digital literacy clubs
  • Safe spaces for homework and mentoring
  • Scholarships for girls in school
  • Integrated health & learning camps
Powered by

AI for Social Good

Using AI to extend human care — never to replace it.

  • Local-language chatbot for mothers’ questions
  • AI tutor for personalised practice for children
  • Data tools to target high-need communities
  • Impact dashboards for donors & partners

Technology

How AI fits into our work.

AI is a tool, not the hero. Doctors, nurses, teachers and community workers remain at the centre of everything we do.

  • AI pregnancy assistant: A simple, verified chatbot that helps mothers understand symptoms, danger signs and nutrition guidance — and encourages timely visits to a clinic.
  • Personalised learning for kids: Adaptive practice exercises that adjust to each child’s pace, offering feedback in their language.
  • Data-informed decisions: We use anonymised data to identify high-need areas, track engagement and measure impact transparently.
Our principles for AI use:
  • Human-first, AI-assisted.
  • Explainable & accountable systems.
  • Privacy and dignity of families come first.
  • Co-designed with doctors, educators & communities.
